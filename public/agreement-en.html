1. General Provisions

1.1. This User Agreement (hereinafter referred to as the "Agreement") regulates the relationship between COUBE LLP, hereinafter referred to as the "Administration," and any individual, sole proprietor, or legal entity, hereinafter referred to as the "User," regarding the use of the COUBE service.

1.2. The COUBE service (hereinafter referred to as the "Service") is software available to the User via a web application or mobile application, designed for placing cargo transportation orders and offers for their execution.

1.3. By starting to use the Service, the User confirms that they have read, understood, and agree to comply with the terms of this Agreement, and also confirms their legal capacity. In case of disagreement with the terms of the Agreement, the use of the Service must be immediately discontinued.

2. Registration and Account

2.1. To use all the functions of the Service, the User needs to go through the registration procedure and create an account.

2.2. During registration, the User undertakes to provide accurate and complete information about themselves and, if necessary, about the legal entity they represent.

2.3. The User is responsible for the security of their account data and for all actions performed using their account.

2.4. The Administration has the right to verify the information provided by the User and, in case of detection of inaccurate data, to suspend or terminate the User's access to the Service.

3. Rights and Obligations of the User

3.1. The User has the right to:

  • Place orders for cargo transportation or offers for their execution;
  • Use the functionality of the Service in accordance with its purpose;
  • Contact the Administration with questions and suggestions regarding the operation of the Service.

3.2. The User undertakes to:

  • Comply with the terms of this Agreement;
  • Not share their account data with third parties;
  • Not use the Service to post information that contradicts the legislation of the Republic of Kazakhstan;
  • Not post false, misleading information;
  • Not perform actions aimed at disrupting the normal operation of the Service.

4. Rights and Obligations of the Administration

4.1. The Administration has the right to:

  • Change the terms of this Agreement unilaterally, notifying the User by posting a new version of the Agreement on the Service;
  • Restrict or terminate the User's access to the Service in case of violation of the terms of the Agreement;
  • Moderate information posted by Users and delete it in case of non-compliance with the terms of the Agreement;
  • Request additional information from the User to confirm their identity or legal status.

4.2. The Administration undertakes to:

  • Ensure the functioning of the Service in accordance with its purpose;
  • Ensure the confidentiality of information received from the User, except for cases provided for by the legislation of the Republic of Kazakhstan;
  • Provide information support to Users on issues related to the operation of the Service.

5.2. The Administration is not responsible for the actions of Users, including the quality of services provided by some Users to others.

5.3. The Service is provided "as is," the Administration does not guarantee that the Service will meet all the User's requirements, function uninterrupted and error-free.

5.4. The Administration is not responsible for any direct or indirect damages resulting from the use or inability to use the Service.

6. Intellectual Property

6.1. All rights to the Service, including design, texts, graphic images, program code, and other elements, belong to the Administration.

6.2. The User is granted a non-exclusive right to use the Service in accordance with its functional purpose.

7. Final Provisions

7.1. This Agreement comes into force from the moment the User starts using the Service and is valid indefinitely.

7.2. Issues not regulated by this Agreement are governed by the current legislation of the Republic of Kazakhstan.

7.3. In case of disputes between the Administration and the User, they are resolved through negotiations, and if it is impossible to reach an agreement - in court at the location of the Administration.

7.4. The Administration has the right at any time to make changes to this Agreement by posting a new version on the Service. Continued use of the Service after making changes means the User's agreement with the new version of the Agreement.
