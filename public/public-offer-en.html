1. General Provisions

1.1. This document is a public offer (proposal) from COUBE LLP (hereinafter referred to as the "Contractor") to conclude a contract for the provision of information services (hereinafter referred to as the "Contract") on the terms set out below.

1.2. In accordance with paragraph 3 of Article 396 of the Civil Code of the Republic of Kazakhstan, this offer is considered accepted (accepted) by the addressee of the offer (hereinafter referred to as the "Customer") from the moment of performing actions indicating their intention to accept the offer, in particular, from the moment of registration of the Customer on the COUBE Service and/or using the functions of the Service.

2. Subject of the Contract

2.1. The Contractor undertakes to provide the Customer with information services consisting in providing access to the COUBE software (hereinafter referred to as the "Service"), and the Customer undertakes to accept and pay for these services.

2.2. The Service is software available through a website and/or mobile application, allowing the Customer to place orders for cargo transportation and receive offers from carriers to fulfill these orders.

2.3. The Contractor does not participate in the conclusion and execution of contracts between the Customer and carriers and is not responsible for the fulfillment of their obligations.

3. Rights and Obligations of the Parties

3.1. The Contractor undertakes to:

  • Provide the Customer with access to the Service after registration;
  • Ensure the functioning of the Service in accordance with its purpose;
  • Ensure the confidentiality of information provided by the Customer, except for cases provided for by the legislation of the Republic of Kazakhstan;
  • Inform the Customer about changes in the operation of the Service.

3.2. The Contractor has the right to:

  • Change the terms of this offer unilaterally, notifying the Customer by posting a new version on the Service;
  • Temporarily suspend the operation of the Service for maintenance;
  • Block the Customer's access to the Service in case of violation of the terms of this Contract.

3.3. The Customer undertakes to:

  • Provide accurate information during registration and use of the Service;
  • Not share their account data with third parties;
  • Not use the Service to post information that contradicts the legislation of the Republic of Kazakhstan;
  • Pay for the Contractor's services in a timely manner in accordance with the tariffs;
  • Independently ensure the confidentiality of their account data and be responsible for all actions performed using their account.

3.4. The Customer has the right to:

  • Use the Service in accordance with its functional purpose;
  • Receive technical support on issues related to the operation of the Service;
  • Stop using the Service at any time.

4. Cost of Services and Payment Procedure

4.1. The cost of the Contractor's services is determined in accordance with the tariffs posted on the Service.

4.2. The Contractor has the right to unilaterally change the tariffs, notifying the Customer by posting information on the Service at least 7 (seven) calendar days before the introduction of new tariffs.

4.3. Payment for services is made by the Customer in the manner and by the methods specified on the Service.

4.4. The date of payment is considered to be the date of receipt of funds to the Contractor's account.

5. Responsibility of the Parties

5.1. For non-fulfillment or improper fulfillment of obligations under this Contract, the parties bear responsibility in accordance with the legislation of the Republic of Kazakhstan.

5.2. The Contractor is not responsible for:

  • Temporary inoperability of the Service for reasons beyond the control of the Contractor;
  • Actions or inaction of carriers with whom the Customer concludes contracts using the Service;
  • Any indirect losses, including lost profits, data loss, or interruption of activities that occurred as a result of the use or inability to use the Service.

5.3. The Customer bears full responsibility for the accuracy and legality of the information posted by them on the Service.

6. Term of the Contract and Termination Procedure

6.1. This Contract comes into force from the moment the offer is accepted by the Customer and is valid indefinitely.

6.2. Either party has the right to unilaterally refuse to fulfill this Contract by notifying the other party.

6.3. In case of termination of the Contract at the initiative of the Customer, the latter is obliged to pay for the services actually rendered to them.

7. Dispute Resolution Procedure

7.1. All disputes and disagreements that may arise between the parties on issues that have not found their resolution in the text of this Contract will be resolved through negotiations.

7.2. In case of impossibility to resolve disputes through negotiations, they are subject to resolution in court in accordance with the legislation of the Republic of Kazakhstan at the location of the Contractor.

8. Final Provisions

8.1. In all other matters not provided for by this Contract, the parties are guided by the legislation of the Republic of Kazakhstan.

8.2. The Contractor has the right at any time to make changes to this offer. The changes come into force from the moment they are posted on the Service, unless a different deadline is specified by the Contractor.

8.3. The Customer undertakes to independently track changes in this offer. Continued use of the Service after making changes to the offer means the Customer's agreement with the new version of the offer.
