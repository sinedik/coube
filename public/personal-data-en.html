Hereby, being a user of the COUBE service (hereinafter referred to as the Service), I voluntarily and knowingly give my consent to COUBE LLP (hereinafter referred to as the Operator) to process the personal data provided by me.

1. List of personal data for which consent is given

1.1. I give consent to the processing of the following personal data:

  • First name, last name, patronymic;
  • Date of birth;
  • Contact information (phone, email address);
  • Identity document data (if necessary);
  • Information about the place of work and position (for representatives of legal entities);
  • Registration address and/or actual residence address;
  • Data on preferences and interests related to the use of the Service;
  • History of interaction with the Service;
  • Other information that I provide when using the Service.

1.2. I also give consent to the processing of technical information that is automatically transmitted by the device through which I use the Service, including IP address, cookie information, browser data, access time, geolocation, and other data on technical interaction with the Service.

2. Purposes of personal data processing

2.1. I give consent to the processing of my personal data for the following purposes:

  • Registration and authentication in the Service;
  • Providing the functionality of the Service in accordance with its purpose;
  • Processing and fulfilling cargo transportation orders;
  • Communication with me on issues related to the use of the Service;
  • Improving the quality of the Service, convenience of its use;
  • Conducting statistical and marketing research;
  • Sending informational and advertising messages about the functionality of the Service, special offers, and promotions (with my separate consent);
  • Fulfilling the requirements of the legislation of the Republic of Kazakhstan.

3. List of actions with personal data

3.1. I give consent to perform the following actions with my personal data: collection, systematization, accumulation, storage, clarification (update, change), extraction, use, transfer (provision, access), blocking, deletion, destruction.

3.2. Processing of personal data can be carried out both with the use of automation tools and without their use.

4. Term of consent

4.1. This consent is valid for the entire period of my use of the Service, as well as for 5 (five) years after the termination of the use of the Service, unless a longer period is provided for by the legislation of the Republic of Kazakhstan.

5. Transfer of personal data to third parties

5.1. I give consent to the transfer of my personal data to third parties in the following cases:

  • To carriers and other users of the Service to the extent necessary for the fulfillment of cargo transportation orders;
  • To service providers who ensure the functioning of the Service (for example, hosting providers, payment systems);
  • To government authorities in cases provided for by the legislation of the Republic of Kazakhstan;
  • In other cases when such transfer is necessary for the functioning of the Service or required by law.

6. Rights of the personal data subject

6.1. I have the right to:

  • Receive information about the processing of my personal data;
  • Request clarification, blocking, or destruction of my personal data in cases provided for by legislation;
  • Withdraw this consent by sending an appropriate notification to the Operator, while the Operator has the right to continue processing personal data without my consent in cases provided for by the legislation of the Republic of Kazakhstan;
  • Exercise other rights provided for by the legislation of the Republic of Kazakhstan.

7. Final provisions

7.1. This consent is valid from the moment it is provided (registration in the Service, use of the Service) and can be withdrawn by me at any time by sending an appropriate notification to the Operator at the email address: info@coube.kz or through the feedback form on the Service.

7.2. In case of withdrawal of consent, the Operator stops processing my personal data and destroys them within 30 (thirty) days from the moment of receiving the withdrawal, unless otherwise provided for by legislation.

7.3. I confirm that, by giving this consent, I act freely, by my own will, and in my own interest.

7.4. I confirm that I have read the Operator's Privacy Policy posted on the Service.
